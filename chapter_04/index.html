css3背景

background五个属性(3之前版本就有的属性)：

background-color属性

background-image属性

background-repeat属性

background-position属性

background-attachment属性

background用法：

background: [background-color] [,background-image] [,background-repeat] [,background-attachment] [,background-position]

background-color属性：

background-color: transparent || color

color可选值：颜色名:red || rgb(255,0,0)或(100%,0%,0%) || hsl(0,100%,50%) || 十六进制#fffeee || css3中可用：rgba(255,0,0,.3) || hsla(0,100%,50%,.3)

background-image属性：

background-image: none || url(path)

background-repeat属性：

background-repeat: no-repeat || repeat || repeat-x || repeat-y

background-atachment属性：

background-atachment: scroll || fixed

background-position属性：

background-position: percentage || length || [left | center | right] [,top | center | bottom]
